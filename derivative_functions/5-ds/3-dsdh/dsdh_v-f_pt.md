Function Call “=H2o.dSdH\_V(pressure, temperature, units)”

**Input Parameters**

- Pressure: (0)MPa, (1)psia, (2)bara, (3)kPa
- Temperature: (0)K, (1)°F, (2)°C, (3)°C
- Units: (0)SI, (1)US Customary, (2)Metric bara, (3) Metric kPa

**Return Value**

- (∂S/∂H)<sub>V</sub>: (0)\[kJ/(kg·K)\]/(kJ/kg), (1)\[BTU/(lbm·°R)\]/(BTU/lbm), (2)\[kJ/(kg·°C)\]/(kJ/kg), (3)\[kJ/(kg·°C)\]/(kJ/kg)

Default values: Units: (0)SI
